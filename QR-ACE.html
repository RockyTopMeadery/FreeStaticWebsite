QR Code Entry for "Ace 'n the Holler"

This is a time-honered favorite recipe here at RTM.

We take the finest buckwheat honey, the richest grade a amber maple syrup, and produce this mead for your enjoyment. Well, ours too... because there's usually a little bit left over.

There's a very faint hint of citrus in there somewhere.

Once the fermenting is done, this mead sits for 3 months in bourbon oak barrels, getting ready to go home with you.

We suggest serving this beauty ever so slightly chilled in a highball or glencairn glass, like what you might drink bourbon from... but this mead is a whole lot smoother than whiskey.
